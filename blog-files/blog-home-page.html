Pros and Cons of Citizen Journalism

Citizen journalism, like many aspects of life, has its pros and cons. On the positive side, citizen journalism has allowed the public a platform where they can fact check the news given out to them by traditional news outlets. This provides multiple perspectives towards a single topic, which can lead to constructive discussions among the public.
